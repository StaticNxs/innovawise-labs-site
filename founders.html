About Jason

Jason Knapp is the founder and lead systems architect behind InnovaWise Labs. He built the company to solve a problem he saw constantly in service businesses: the work is there, the demand is there, but the systems holding it together are running on memory, spreadsheets, and whoever happens to pick up the phone.

Every engagement is led by Jason directly, from the first conversation about where a business is losing time or money, through the workflow build, to the result you can actually measure. There's no handoff to a delivery team you've never spoken with.

That direct involvement is intentional. When you're the one building the system, you're also the one accountable for whether it actually works.
