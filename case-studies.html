The Challenge

A regional logistics firm with 60 employees was spending 22+ hours per week manually processing inbound freight documentation, cross-referencing client records, and routing exceptions through email chains. The process was error-prone, delayed billing cycles, and consumed the time of their most experienced operations staff.

The Solution

We deployed an AI-powered document processing pipeline using the AxentraWise™ framework — automating document intake, intelligent data extraction, cross-system validation, and exception routing. The entire workflow now runs overnight, unattended, with automated alerts for edge cases that genuinely require human judgment.

The Outcome

The operations team hasn't touched the process in three months. Billing cycle time decreased by 40%. Exception rates dropped by 78% due to validation logic catching upstream errors before they propagated.

The Challenge

A 45-person SaaS platform had landed two enterprise prospects who required SOC 2 Type II compliance as a vendor prerequisite. With no formal security program in place and 90 days until client review, they needed a systematic path from zero to audit-ready — fast.

The Solution

We conducted a comprehensive gap assessment against SOC 2 Trust Service Criteria, designed and implemented required technical controls, deployed continuous monitoring and logging infrastructure, and developed the policies and procedures required for the audit narrative.

The Outcome

SOC 2 readiness achieved in 60 days. Both enterprise clients approved the security review. The platform closed $1.2M in new ARR directly attributable to the compliance posture they could now demonstrate.
