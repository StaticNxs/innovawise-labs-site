FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers about what we build, how it works, and what to expect — no jargon, no sales spin.

What does InnovaWise Labs actually do?

We design and install practical AI-powered workflows for service businesses — handling things like missed-call recovery, lead and quote follow-up, invoice reminders, customer response, and admin work that's currently being done by hand. We're not a chatbot vendor or a generic software reseller. We build the specific system your business needs and connect it to the tools you already use.

What is an AI Profit System?

An AI Profit System is a workflow we install that's tied to a measurable business result — faster response time, more completed follow-up, fewer missed invoices, less manual admin work. Instead of selling you a platform and hoping it gets used, we build around a specific operational leak and track whether it actually closes.

Is InnovaWise Labs an AI agency?

No. AI agencies typically sell chatbots, prompts, or generic automation templates. We build and install real operational workflows — connected to your phones, your CRM, your invoicing, and your team's actual day-to-day work — using AI as one part of the system, not the whole pitch.

Do you only work with certain types of businesses?

We focus on owner-led service businesses — contractors, electricians, plumbers, painters, landscapers, roofers, real estate teams, and similar operations — where leads, calls, quotes, and invoices are still managed manually. If your business runs on phone calls, spreadsheets, and inboxes, we're built for you.

Will this replace the software we already use?

Usually not. Most of the value comes from connecting and automating the tools you already have — your CRM, phone system, invoicing software, calendar — rather than ripping them out and replacing them. We only recommend new tools when your current stack genuinely can't support the workflow.

What is the AxentraWise™ framework, and do I need to understand it?

AxentraWise™ is the framework we use internally to find operational leaks, prioritize the right fix, install the workflow, and measure the result. You don't need to learn it or manage it — it's simply the standard we apply to every engagement so the work stays consistent and measurable. Explore AxentraWise™ →

What happens during the first audit?

Your free AI Profit Leak Audit is a focused, 90-minute conversation about how your business actually operates today — where calls, leads, quotes, and invoices are getting stuck. You'll leave with a specific recommendation for the first workflow worth fixing, what it would take, and what to expect. No generic sales pitch.

How long does it take to get a system live?

Basic automations — like missed-call recovery or invoice reminders — typically go live in 5 to 7 days. Larger framework deployments that touch multiple workflows generally run 6 to 12 weeks, depending on scope and how many systems need to connect.

Are we locked into a long-term contract?

No. You own the systems we build. We don't create dependencies designed to trap you into ongoing contracts. If you decide to stay, it's because the system is working — not because you have no way out.

Do you help with SOC 2, HIPAA, or PCI DSS compliance?

When these requirements are relevant to your business, InnovaWise Labs can design and automate the operational workflows that make compliance readiness, evidence collection, assessments, and ongoing control management more organized and less manual.

Our support may include:

  • Secure automation implementation
  • User access and offboarding workflows
  • Evidence collection automation
  • Vendor-management workflows
  • Policy acknowledgement tracking
  • Audit and assessment request tracking
  • Compliance dashboards
  • Security-questionnaire support
  • Documentation and readiness project management

We provide implementation and readiness support — not legal advice, formal audits, attestations, certifications, penetration testing, continuous security monitoring, incident response, or guarantees of compliance. Final compliance decisions, examinations, and validations remain with your organization and the appropriate auditors, assessors, legal counsel, regulators, acquiring banks, or payment partners.
